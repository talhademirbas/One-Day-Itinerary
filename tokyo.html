One Day Itinerary

If you only have one day to spend in Tokyo, here's a suggested itinerary:

  • 9:00am - Start your day at the Tsukiji Fish Market, where you can witness the famous tuna auction and enjoy fresh sushi for breakfast.
  • 11:00am - Visit the Asakusa Shrine and the nearby Senso-ji Temple, which are some of the oldest and most famous religious sites in Tokyo.
  • 1:00pm - Take a stroll in the Ueno Park and visit the Tokyo National Museum to learn about Japanese art and history.
  • 3:00pm - Explore the Shibuya Crossing, one of the busiest intersections in the world, and shop in the nearby Shibuya Hikarie shopping complex.
  • 6:00pm - Take the Tokyo Skytree elevator to the observation deck and enjoy the panoramic view of Tokyo at sunset.
  • 8:00pm - End your day with a traditional Japanese dinner at a ryotei, a high-end Japanese restaurant known for its exquisite cuisine and hospitality.

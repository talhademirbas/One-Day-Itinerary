One Day Itinerary

If you only have one day to spend in New York City, here's a suggested itinerary:

  • 9:00am - Start your day at the Central Park. Take a walk around the park and enjoy the scenery.
  • 11:00am - Head to the American Museum of Natural History. Spend a few hours exploring the exhibits.
  • 2:00pm - Take the subway to 9/11 Memorial and Museum to pay tribute to the victims of the 9/11 attacks and learn about the history of the events.
  • 4:00pm - Take a stroll down Wall Street to see the famous Charging Bull and the New York Stock Exchange.
  • 6:00pm - Head to Brooklyn Bridge Park to enjoy a scenic view of the Manhattan skyline at sunset.
  • 8:00pm - End your day at one of the many great restaurants in New York City, such as Keens Steakhouse or Gramercy Tavern.

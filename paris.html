One Day Itinerary

If you only have one day to spend in Paris, here's a suggested itinerary:

  • 9:00am - Start your day at the Louvre Museum. Spend a few hours exploring the museum's world-renowned collection.
  • 12:00pm - Head to the Notre-Dame Cathedral and climb the towers for a breathtaking view of Paris.
  • 2:00pm - Take a stroll down the Champs-Élysées and visit the Arc de Triomphe.
  • 4:00pm - Visit the Eiffel Tower and enjoy the panoramic view of Paris from the top.
  • 6:00pm - Head to the Montmartre neighborhood and explore the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  • 8:00pm - End your day at a traditional Parisian restaurant, such as Chez Julien or La Poularde.
